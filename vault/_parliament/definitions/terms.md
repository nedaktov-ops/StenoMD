## Legislative Terms

### Proiect de lege (Government Bill)
**Definition**: Draft law submitted by the Government (through Prime Minister)
**Key Feature**: Most common type of legislation
**Example**: "Proiect de Lege privind bugetul de stat"

### Propunere legislativă (Parliamentary Proposal)
**Definition**: Draft law submitted by Deputies or Senators
**Key Feature**: Can be proposed by individual or group of MPs
**Example**: "Propune legislativă pentru modificarea..."

### Inițiativă cetățenească (Citizens' Initiative)  
**Definition**: Law proposed by citizens with 100,000+ signatures
**Limitation**: Cannot cover fiscal matters, amnesty, or pardon

### Lege (Law)
**Definition**: Legislation adopted by Parliament and promulgated by President
**Publication**: Enters force upon publication in Monitorul Oficial
**Example**: "Legea 24/2024"

### Hotărâre (Decision)
**Definition**: Parliamentary decision on internal matters (not substantive law)
**Example**: "Hotărâre privind regulamentul..."

### Ordonanță de urgență (Emergency Ordinance)
**Definition**: Government emergency legislation, requires Parliament approval
**Note**: Can be struck down as unconstitutional

### Procedură de urgență (Emergency Procedure)
**Definition**: Faster legislative pathway for urgent matters
**Effect**: Shorter debate periods in committees/plenary

## Parliamentary Structure

### Grup parlamentar (Parliamentary Group)
**Definition**: Official political structure within each chamber
**Requirement**: Minimum 10 members elected on same party list
**Chair**: Elected group leader
**Rights**: Committee seats, speaking rights, Bureau representation

### Grupuri mixte (Mixed Groups)
**Definition**: Groups formed by parties below threshold + independents
**Example**: "Deputați neafiliați"

### Biroul Permanent (Bureau)
**Definition**: Leadership structure of each Chamber
**Members**: President, Vice-Presidents, Secretaries, Quaestors

### Comisie permanentă (Standing Committee)
**Definition**: Specialized policy committee for review
**Senate**: 15 committees
**Chamber**: 17 committees

## Electoral Terms

### Circumscripție electorală (Electoral Constituency)
**Definition**: Electoral district for elections
**Romania**: 41 counties + Bucharest + diaspora

### Lista electorală (Electoral List)
**Definition**: Party list for proportional representation
**Order**: Determines who gets elected from party

### Supleant (Stand-in)
**Definition**: Backup candidate if elected member resigns
**Note**: Takes seat if original resigns

## Traseism Related

### Traseism parlamentar (Floor-Crossing)
**Definition**: Changing party affiliation after election
**CPC Definition**: More than 2 parties in political career
**Legal Status**: LEGAL for parliamentarians (no restriction)

### Partid original (Original Party)
**Definition**: Party on whose list MP was elected
**Importance**: Traseism calculation baseline

### Grup parlamentar actual (Current Group)
**Definition**: Parliamentary group MP belongs to currently
**Note**: Can differ from party if formed new group

## Process Terms

### Cameră decizională (Decisional Chamber)
**Definition**: Chamber that makes final decision on law
**Rule**: Senate for most matters, Chamber for treaties/specific laws

### Promulgare (Promulgation)
**Definition**: Presidential approval of adopted law
**Timeframe**: 20 days after adoption

### Monitorul Oficial (Official Gazette)
**Definition**: Official publication where laws are published
**Part I**: Laws (entry into force)
**Part II**: Stenograms
**Note**: Law enters force upon publication or specified date
