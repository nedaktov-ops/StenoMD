## Legend

- **Senate (Senat)**: 137 senators elected by proportional representation
- **Chamber of Deputies (Camera Deputaților)**: 330 deputies
- **Joint Sessions**: Rare combined sessions for specific matters (e.g., constitutional revisions)

## Key Facts

- **Legislature**: 4 years (2024-2028 current)
- **Total Parliament**: 467 members
- **Election System**: Proportional representation with party lists
- **Constitution**: 1991 (revised 2003)
