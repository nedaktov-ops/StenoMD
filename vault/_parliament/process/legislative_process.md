# Legislative Process in Romania

## Overview

The Romanian Parliament uses a **bicameral** system:
- **Senat (Senate)** - Upper chamber
- **Camera Deputaților (Chamber of Deputies)** - Lower chamber

## Who Can Initiate Legislation

| Initiator Type | Description | Requirements |
|---------------|-------------|-------------|
| Guvern | Government | Cabinet proposal |
| Senator | Individual Senator | Parliamentary member |
| Deputy | Individual Deputy | Parliamentary member |  
| Citizens | People's Initiative | 250,000+ valid signatures |

## Process Stages

1. **Legislative Initiative** - Proposal submitted to Parliament Secretariat
2. **Committee Review** - Permanent committees examine the proposal
3. **Committee Report** - Report submitted to plenary
4. **Plenary Debate** - General debate in chamber
5. **Voting** - Vote on the proposal
6. **Second Chamber** - Same process in other chamber
7. **Mediation** - If chambers disagree
8. **Constitutional Review** - CCR review
9. **Promulgation** - President signs
10. **Publication** - Monitorul Oficial

## Law Types

| Type | Voting Requirement | Examples |
|------|----------------|----------|
| Organic | Majority of ALL members | Constitutional matters, courts |
| Ordinary | Majority of PRESENT members | Most laws |
| Constitutional Revision | 2/3 of ALL members | Constitution changes |

## Key Concepts

- **Proiect de lege** - Bill (Government proposal)
- **Propunere legislativă** - Legislative proposal (from MPs/citizens)
- **Inițiativă legislativă** - Legislative initiative
- **Lege** - Law (adopted act)
